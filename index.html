How it works

A process you can set a watch to.

  1. T−4

    Discovery

    A call to understand the goal, the audience, and the constraints. You get a written scope and a fixed quote within 48 hours.

  2. T−3

    Design

    Wireframes first, then full designs. You review real pages in the browser — not static mockups — and nothing moves forward without sign-off.

  3. T−2

    Build

    Weekly progress links you can click through. Accessibility, performance, and security checks run before anything is called done.

  4. T−0

    Launch & support

    Deployment, handover documentation, and 30 days of included support. After that, a maintenance plan if you want one — no lock-in.
